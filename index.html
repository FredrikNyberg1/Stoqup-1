What is Stoqup?

Stoqup is the future of oil based packaging. We believe that the next generation of oil based packaging lies here with us, come along and help us decrease the environment mark on our planet.

The new innovative container for oil based products

Comparing Stoqup packaging to glass based packaging there is a clear difference in emissions, in the environmental mark and in the cost. Recycling Stoqup is also easier for the average consumer and the energy required to recycle is way less compared to glass products. With Stoqup we can help make our planet a better but we need your help to make that happen!

Benefits

Transportable

The bag-in-box bottle doesn’t need any extra padded protection during shipping, perfect for eCommerce. Stoqup's robust and virtually unbreakable design makes it stackable and easy to distribute from warehouse to consumer.

Profitable

A 250ml bag-in-box bottle weights just 283 gm when fully filled, compared to 525 gm for glass. B2B or B2C, you’re going to save money! Flat-pack design occupies 80% less space than empty glass bottles and weighs up to 95% less, representing a significant reduction in your procurement costs.

Sustainable

Stoqup translates into a significantly lower carbon footprint due to it's lighter design and easier to transport product packaging. Our unique re-usable tap & box enables the shift towards a more circular economy.

Brandable

The entire surface of the package - all four sides and even the top, can be used to leverage your brand and allows you to be more creative.

Psst...did you know that 7 out of 10 consumers actually prefer paper-based packaging over glass and plastic bottles?
